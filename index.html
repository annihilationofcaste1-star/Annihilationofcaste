About the text

A speech that was never delivered — and changed everything.

Ambedkar was invited to address the Jat-Pat-Todak Mandal — a Hindu reformist group in Lahore. When the organizers read his speech in advance, they cancelled the event. Ambedkar published it himself. It became one of the most important political texts of the 20th century.
