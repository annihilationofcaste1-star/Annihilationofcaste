Undelivered · 1936 Dr. B.R. Ambedkar

Annihilation of Caste

The speech they tried to silence — now a living archive for those who refuse to look away.

Ambedkar wrote this speech to be delivered at a Hindu reform conference. The organisers cancelled the event when they read it. He published it himself. This site exists so that what they tried to suppress reaches everyone. Read the text. Take the Mirror Test. See the evidence. Begin unlearning.

01 The numbers they'd rather you not see

Caste is not old-fashioned.
It is the present tense.

For those who claim caste discrimination is "a thing of the past," here are the numbers — from the Government of India's own reporting. Every figure below is from official sources.

57,582
Crimes against Scheduled Castes registered in 2022 alone
Source · NCRB 2022
27.4%
Conviction rate under the SC/ST Atrocities Act — proving under-enforcement, not misuse
Source · NCRB 2022
50k+
People still engaged in manual scavenging — banned by law since 1993
Source · Safai Karmachari Andolan
1.8lac
SC/ST atrocity cases pending trial — the second injustice of judicial delay

How casteist are you really?

Most people think the answer is "not at all."

The Mirror Test covers fifteen scenarios across two dimensions — personal bias and systemic denial. It includes questions most people have never been asked to sit with: what do you think when you see a sanitation worker's child on your sofa? How do you respond when someone cites a friendship as proof caste is over?

Every answer reveals the research behind it. Your result plots on a two-axis compass, showing where you actually stand — not where you think you do.
